Fresh Start Behavioral Health, Inc.

"Fresh Start" is more than just our name, it's also our philosophy. Led by medical director Irfan Dahar, MD,  executive director Ebenezer Aluma, Ph.D., PA-C, and our experienced clinical leadership team, our team provide personalized substance abuse and mental health treatments so you can take back control of your life. Our model incorporates psychiatric interventions, counseling, behavioral therapy, and sober living to equip you with the tools you need to live a healthier, fulfilled life. Reach out to our providers in Dayton, or Milford, OH today to start your healing journey.

Your Partners in Mental Health

Our Team

Our team comprises a wide variety of seasoned, mental and behavioral  health providers including psychiatrists, physician assistants, nurse practitioners,  social workers, licensed professional clinical counselors,  licensed drug and chemical dependence counselors, chemical dependency counseling assistants, qualified behavioral health specialists and allied professionals who bring a wealth of experience in addiction medicine and mental health treatments. We emphasize the importance of open communication, mutual respect, trust, compassion, and empathy for every patient we work with, tailoring every therapy plan to meet the needs of each individual. Above all, we strive to create a welcoming, nurturing environment for you to get past your challenges and move forward with your life.
